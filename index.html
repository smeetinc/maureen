Hello and welcome! I'm a final-year student at the University of Szczecin, majoring in economics and IT applications. I've served as a Financial Officer and Purchasing Officer in retail and wholesale industries, gaining invaluable insights into finance and operations.

Currently, I'm enhancing my skill set as a Customer Service Agent with Ryanair in Poland. This role has sharpened my focus on customer-centric solutions and efficient communication strategies. In addition to my professional endeavors, I possess key traits such as self-reliance, confidence, and a proactive learning attitude. I consistently approach challenges with curiosity and determination, viewing each experience as an opportunity for growth. Outside of work and studies, I enjoy traveling, cooking, singing, and embracing new adventures. These passions not only fuel my personal growth but also reflect my enthusiasm for life and continuous learning. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I look forward to connecting, collaborating, and exploring new opportunities together.
